Who is Virat Kohli?

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a former captain of the Indian cricket team and one of the best batsmen in the world. Kohli is known for his extraordinary consistency, aggressive captaincy, and match-winning performances. Over the years, he has become the face of Indian cricket, breaking numerous records across all formats of the game.

As a batsman, Kohli has been unparalleled, scoring over 70 international centuries, a feat achieved by only a few in cricketing history. He holds numerous records, including being the fastest to 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 ODI runs. His passion for fitness has inspired a revolution in Indian cricket's approach to physical training.

Off the field, Virat is known for his philanthropy and his dedication to social causes. Alongside his wife, Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, he has been involved in initiatives promoting child welfare and animal rights.
